REQUIREMENTS FROM THE SECOND YEAR TO THE THIRD YEAR OF HIGH SCHOOL
1.- COPY OF THE COLOR CARD OF THE STUDENT.
2.- COPY OF COLOR ID OF THE FATHER AND MOTHER.
3.- GRADE REPORT FOR THE FIRST QUARTER OF THIS SCHOOL YEAR 2022-2023.

REQUIREMENTS FOR INITIAL EDUCATION 2
UBLEVEL 1: For girls and boys from 3 to 4 years old
SUBLEVEL 2: For girls and boys from 4 to 5 years old
1.- COPY OF THE COLOR CARD OF THE STUDENT
2.- COPY OF COLOR ID OF THE FATHER AND MOTHER.
NOTE:
-INITIAL 2 STUDENTS, SUBLEVEL 1, MUST BE 3 YEARS OLD UNTIL THE FIRST DAY OF CLASS.
-INITIAL 2 STUDENTS, SUBLEVEL 2, MUST BE 4 YEARS OLD UNTIL THE FIRST DAY OF CLASS.

HIGH SCHOOL REQUIREMENTS (FIRST OF EGB)
1.- COPY OF THE COLOR CARD OF THE STUDENT
2.- COPY OF COLOR ID OF THE FATHER AND MOTHER
3.- INITIAL LEARNING REPORT 2 SUB LEVEL 2
NOTE:
- HIGH SCHOOL STUDENTS (FIRST YEAR OF BASIC), MUST BE 5 YEARS OLD BY THE FIRST DAY OF CLASS.
