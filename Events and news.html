Manual

SCHOLARSHIP APPLICATION PROCEDURE MANUAL 2022 -2023

The Nuestra Señora de Fátima Educational Unit is an educational establishment with seventy-four years of existence, whose function has not only been framed in cognitively structuring children and adolescents, but also in guiding Christian and moral values.
The Educational Unit "NUESTRA SEÑORA DE FÁTIMA" through this private instrument, regulates the public service it provides to users and guarantees the applicability of the Instructions provided by the National Educational Authority. Non-reimbursable financing is called a "scholarship", which is intended to finance the cost of pension and enrollment values ​​at the Initial, Basic and Baccalaureate for those students with limited financial resources.
